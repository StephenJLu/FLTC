To our esteemed members, partners, and the forensic community,

It is with a heavy heart that I announce the decision to close Forensic Leaders Training Center®. Since our founding, we have been a private company with a not-for-profit mentality, aiming to reinvest the majority of our profits back into services and support for our members. Our mission has always been to elevate the leadership skills of forensic professionals across the United States, recognizing that strong leadership is the foundation of integrity and effectiveness within forensic laboratories.

Our purpose has been clear: to positively influence the working culture of forensic professionals and drive continuous improvement in managing laboratories, teams, and units. Through our community, leadership programs, and resources, we have provided tools for professional growth for forensic professionals. Together, we have made strides toward a workplace culture rooted in integrity, collaboration, and excellence.

The decision to close was not made lightly. We are deeply grateful for each member, partner, and colleague who has joined us on this journey. Your commitment to the public good and your pursuit of leadership excellence has been inspiring, and the collective achievements we have shared are testament to what can be accomplished when dedicated professionals come together.

Although this chapter is ending, we are confident that the leadership, skills, and connections forged here will continue to serve you in all your professional endeavors. Thank you for your dedication to the mission, and for being a part of Forensic Leaders Training Center®.

With heartfelt gratitude,
Stephen J. Lu
Forensic Leaders Training Center®
